Our Mission 
Our mission is to support enhance women professional and personal growth and fulfillment 
We are deeply committed to providing achievement-driven women with Inspiration, Guidance, and Networking 
                    







Our Target Audience
Millennial women pursuing various careers ranging from art and fashion, to finance and politics;
Female professionals who have reached the middle management position and are struggling to break through the glass ceiling;
Women seeking a transition in order to fulfill their potential and receive recognition.

                    







Our Approach
We organize and conduct seminars with women speakers leaders in their industry
 TED Talks for Women in a seminar format
We connect women with mentors in the industries of their interest
